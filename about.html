About

Our Beginnings

It all started in a backyard barbecue party every holiday. The idea came from friends and relatives who are getting addicted to barbecue.

We found a place in a food park however, there's no available space for our business so we convert our garage into small smoker house with few tables.

As time flies, we noticed that our business turned into a big restaurant. This cannot be a success without the people embracing the western BBQ here in Asia.

It's not just meat. It's our canvas.

For us, BBQ is an art. We don't just smoke and grill. We cook it to perfection that your taste buds will appreciate. This is our masterpiece.
